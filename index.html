Rethinking L.A.
A New Architecture for Los Angeles
Scroll
Los Angeles is one of the most desired addresses in the world.

Yet most of its residential architecture is built to the same standard as a tract home.
MONOLITH proposes a radical alternative: homes designed as permanent structures — where architecture is not an expense that depreciates, but an asset that appreciates with time.
This requires a new definition of luxury.
The Vision
Ethical Luxury.
Architecture that earns its value.
Excelling in Los Angeles — home to the most celebrated residences in the world — is an ambitious goal. MONOLITH makes it possible through a unique model: an international collaboration of specialized partners, each a leader in their discipline, united with local experts who bring deep knowledge of California's construction landscape and permitting process.
