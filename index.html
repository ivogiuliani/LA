Safer from Wildfires

The CDI program defines 12 mitigation measures. By law, every insurer must offer discounts per measure adopted. A My Villa meets all 12 natively — by structure, not by retrofit.

All 12 measures as standard
Title 24, Part 7

2026 CA WUI Code

Mandatory from Jan 1, 2026 for all new builds in Fire Hazard Severity Zones. ICF construction achieves native compliance with virtually all structural requirements.

ICF = built-in compliance
Gold standard for insurers

IBHS Wildfire Prepared Home +

The highest voluntary certification recognized by carriers — Mercury, USAA, Travelers, Chubb. Mercury alone offers up to 50% wildfire premium discount for IBHS-certified homes.

Carrier-recognized

How we build it — six layers of defense.

01

ICF Concrete Shell

250mm reinforced-concrete walls — a non-combustible envelope with 4+ hour fire rating. No wood frames, no combustible cladding. The structure itself is the fire barrier.

02

Windows & Vents

Multi-pane tempered glass. SFM-certified WUI-compliant vents on all openings — the most critical ember-entry prevention per IBHS research.

03

Roof & Eaves

Class A fire-rated concrete roof. Enclosed boxed eaves and non-combustible soffits prevent ember accumulation at roof-wall junctions.

04

Sprinkler System

NFPA 13D residential sprinklers as standard. On-site water reserve independent of municipal supply. Insurance ROI in 2–5 years from premium savings alone.

05

Defensible Space

Non-combustible 5-foot Zone 0 perimeter. Fire-smart Mediterranean landscaping — olive, lavender, succulents. IBHS certification prerequisite.

06

Off-Grid Safety

Solar + battery for 2–3 days of autonomy. During PSPS shutoffs and evacuations, sprinklers, pumps and communications stay operational.

Engineered to endure.

Energy-independent, naturally cool, off-grid ready. Our integrated system — developed with Transsolar Klima Engineering and BUROMILAN — combines passive design, renewable energy and fire-resistant construction into a home that protects you, reduces your footprint, and works even when the grid doesn't.

Energy Independence

Integrated PV panels supply 80% of electricity and 20% of hot water. Paired with battery storage, your villa provides its own energy — a 100% electrical system with no gas refrigerants.

Own energy, even off-grid

2–3 Day Off-Grid Autonomy

Water collection during droughts, solar + battery during blackouts, independent electrical systems for emergencies. A self-sufficient refuge when you need it most.

Water + energy reserves

Natural Ventilation & Microclimate

Cross-ventilation calibrated for L.A.'s west-to-east winds. Taller volumes to the south for optimal shading; exposed concrete maximizes thermal mass. Naturally cool — even without electricity.

Cool without AC

Reduced Energy Consumption

Thermal mass, passive shading, green roofs as natural insulation, and cross-ventilation dramatically reduce energy needs. Lower bills, smaller footprint, greater comfort.

IT'S Architecture

IT'S is a research- and innovation-oriented practice founded in 2016 by Alessandro Cambi, Francesco Marinelli and Paolo Mezzalama. Its thesis: architecture as a long-duration object — designed to outlast trends, weather, and market cycles. My Villa is the studio's California practice and response to the climate challenges affecting the city of Los Angeles.
