2026 CA WUI Code

Title 24, Part 7 — mandatory from Jan 1, 2026 for all new builds in Fire Hazard Severity Zones. ICF construction achieves native compliance for virtually all structural requirements.

How We Build It

Six integrated layers of fire protection — from structure to landscape

ICF Concrete Shell

250mm reinforced concrete walls — non-combustible envelope with 4+ hour fire rating. No wood frames, no combustible cladding. The structure itself is the fire barrier.

Windows & Vents

Multi-pane tempered glass. SFM-certified WUI-compliant vents on all openings — the most critical ember-entry prevention measure per IBHS research.

Roof & Eaves

Class A fire-rated concrete roof. Enclosed boxed eaves and non-combustible soffits prevent ember accumulation at roof-wall junctions.

Sprinkler System

NFPA 13D residential sprinklers as standard. On-site water reserve independent of municipal supply. Insurance ROI in 2–5 years from premium savings alone.

Defensible Space

Non-combustible 5-foot Zone 0 perimeter. Fire-smart Mediterranean landscaping — olive, lavender, succulents. IBHS certification prerequisite.

Off-Grid Safety

Solar + battery for 2–3 days autonomy. During PSPS shutoffs and evacuations, sprinklers, pumps and communications stay operational.

### Engineered to Endure

Energy-independent, naturally cool, and off-grid ready.

Every My Villa is engineered to perform in extreme conditions. Our integrated system — developed with Transsolar Klima Engineering and BUROMILAN — combines passive design, renewable energy, and fire-resistant construction into a home that protects you, reduces your footprint, and works even when the grid doesn't.

Energy Independence

Integrated PV panels supply 80% of electricity and 20% of hot water. Paired with battery storage, your villa provides its own energy — even during blackouts. A 100% electrical system that uses no gas refrigerants.

Own energy, even off-grid

2–3 Day Off-Grid Autonomy

Water collection provides supply during droughts. Solar + battery storage ensures power during blackouts. Independent electrical systems and backup power for emergencies. Your home becomes a self-sufficient refuge when you need it most.

Water + energy reserves

Natural Ventilation & Microclimate

Cross-ventilation calibrated for L.A.'s west-to-east winds. Taller volumes to the south for optimal shading. Exposed concrete maximizes thermal mass. Naturally cool environments — even without electricity.
