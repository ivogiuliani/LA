Natural Ventilation & Microclimate
Cross-ventilation calibrated for L.A.'s west-to-east winds. Taller volumes to the south for optimal shading. Exposed concrete maximizes thermal mass. South-facing windows with overhangs. Naturally cool environments — even without electricity.

Why Concrete Homes Cost Less Over 30 Years

A reinforced concrete villa costs more to build — but is designed to cost less to own. In the right conditions, resilient construction can support stronger insurability, lower maintenance exposure, and better long-term value retention than conventional luxury homes.

In comparable markets, resilient concrete construction has been associated with appraisal premiums of up to 6% over conventional wood-frame. Actual outcomes are project- and market-specific.

The Process

From Conversation to Keys in 20 Months

Custom Villa Build Timeline — 20-Month Delivery

Every My Villa follows a proven path — a system refined over decades of Italian construction practice, adapted for California. Fixed timelines, transparent budgets, zero compromise on design.
Month 0 Month 20
01
Discovery Month 1
A private conversation about your vision, your site, and how you live. Our team visits the land, studies orientation, sunlight, prevailing winds, and neighborhood context.
02
Typology Selection Month 2
Choose from four foundational archetypes — Courtyard, Deconstructed, Hill, or Linear — each calibrated for your site's specific geometry and your lifestyle.
03
Personalization Months 2–4
Materials, finishes, spatial adjustments. You shape every detail within a system engineered for beauty and buildability. Fair-faced concrete, Venetian plaster, Italian ceramics — your palette.
04
Engineering & Permits Months 4–7
Structural engineering by our Italian team in collaboration with L.A. specialists. Transsolar models the climate performance. Permits filed with a pre-validated system that accelerates approval.
05
Construction & Delivery Months 7–20
Built by DGU concrete specialists. Weekly progress updates, fixed budget, and a dedicated project manager. You receive your keys to a home built for generations.
