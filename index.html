The Investment

Concrete vs. Wood:
Total Cost of Ownership

A concrete home costs more to build — but less to own. Lower insurance, near-zero maintenance, faster appreciation, and total fire resilience create a compounding financial advantage that widens every year.
Day-1 Premium
+6%
instant appraisal uplift
On day one, a concrete home appraises 6% higher than an identical wood-frame property. A static premium that exists in any market — not just fire zones.
Source: NAR Cost vs Value Report
Annual Growth Spread
+1.5%/yr
faster year-over-year appreciation
In CA fire zones, concrete grows 1.5 points faster every year (5.5% vs 4.0%). Unlike the day-1 premium, this advantage compounds — the gap widens each year you hold.
Source: JRER, CA Assessor data
Insurance Savings
50-72%
lower annual premiums
Fire-hardened homes qualify for up to 50-72% lower premiums in CA fire zones. Over 10 years on a $20M property, savings exceed $600K vs wood-frame.
Source: CA Dept of Insurance, IBHS

The Process

From Conversation to Keys in 18 Months

Every My Villa follows a proven path — a system refined over decades of Italian construction practice, adapted for California. Fixed timelines, transparent budgets, zero compromise on design.
Month 0 Month 18
01
Discovery Month 1
A private conversation about your vision, your site, and how you live. Our team visits the land, studies orientation, sunlight, prevailing winds, and neighborhood context.
02
Typology Selection Month 2
Choose from four foundational archetypes — Courtyard, Deconstructed, Hill, or Linear — each calibrated for your site's specific geometry and your lifestyle.
03
Personalization Months 2–4
Materials, finishes, spatial adjustments. You shape every detail within a system engineered for beauty and buildability. Fair-faced concrete, Venetian plaster, Italian ceramics — your palette.
04
Engineering & Permits Months 4–6
Structural engineering by our Italian team in collaboration with L.A. specialists. Transsolar models the climate performance. Permits filed with a pre-validated system that accelerates approval.
05
Construction & Delivery Months 6–18
Built by DGU concrete specialists. Weekly progress updates, fixed budget, and a dedicated project manager. You receive your keys to a home built for generations.
◆ Fixed price. Fixed timeline. No surprises.
