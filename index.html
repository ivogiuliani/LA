The Process

From Conversation to Keys in 18 Months

Every My Villa follows a proven path — a system refined over decades of Italian construction practice, adapted for California. Fixed timelines, transparent budgets, zero compromise on design.
Month 0 Month 18
01
Discovery Month 1
A private conversation about your vision, your site, and how you live. Our team visits the land, studies orientation, sunlight, prevailing winds, and neighborhood context.
02
Typology Selection Month 2
Choose from four foundational archetypes — Courtyard, Deconstructed, Hill, or Linear — each calibrated for your site's specific geometry and your lifestyle.
03
Personalization Months 2–4
Materials, finishes, spatial adjustments. You shape every detail within a system engineered for beauty and buildability. Fair-faced concrete, Venetian plaster, Italian ceramics — your palette.
04
Engineering & Permits Months 4–6
Structural engineering by our Italian team in collaboration with L.A. specialists. Transsolar models the climate performance. Permits filed with a pre-validated system that accelerates approval.
05
Construction & Delivery Months 6–18
Built by The Cooperative — L.A.'s finest concrete specialists. Weekly progress updates, fixed budget, and a dedicated project manager. You receive your keys to a home built for generations.
◆ Fixed price. Fixed timeline. No surprises.
